💬 What is a WhatsApp Click-to-Chat Link?

A WhatsApp click-to-chat link is a special URL that opens a WhatsApp conversation directly with any phone number — even if the number isn't saved in your contacts. These links use WhatsApp's official wa.me short-link format, and can optionally include a pre-filled message that appears in the chat box the moment it opens.

Businesses, freelancers, and marketers use click-to-chat links on websites, email signatures, social media bios, and digital ads to make it effortless for customers to start a conversation in one tap.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this WhatsApp link generator completely free?
Yes — 100% free, forever. There are no premium plans, no usage limits, and no hidden charges. You can generate as many WhatsApp links as you need without creating an account.
Do I need to save the number to start a chat?
No. That's the entire purpose of a click-to-chat link. When someone clicks the link, WhatsApp opens a conversation with that number directly — without either party needing to save the contact first.
Can I use this for my WhatsApp Business account?
Absolutely. Click-to-chat links work for both personal WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business numbers. Simply enter your business phone number and customize the pre-filled message to match your brand tone.
Is it safe to share my number in a WhatsApp link?
The link contains your phone number in the URL, so only share it with people or platforms you trust. This tool processes everything locally in your browser — we never store, transmit, or log any phone numbers you enter.
What is the correct format for the phone number?
Enter the number without the leading zero, country code, or + symbol. For example, for the Indian number 09876543210, enter 9876543210. The tool automatically prepends the country code you select from the dropdown.
Does the pre-filled message send automatically?
No. The message is pre-filled in the chat input box when WhatsApp opens — but the user still needs to tap the send button to actually send it. This is by WhatsApp's design to maintain user consent.
